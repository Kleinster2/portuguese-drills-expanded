## ğŸ“ Educational Psychology Principles

### 1. Immediate Feedback
- AI responds instantly
- Show corrections with explanation
- Celebrate small wins

### 2. Spaced Repetition
- Review past units periodically
- "Rusty on Unit 2? Quick review?"
- Interleave old and new material

### 3. Low-Stakes Practice
- No grades, just progress
- Make mistakes safely
- Retry unlimited times

### 4. Clear Goal Hierarchy
- Micro: Answer this question
- Meso: Complete this unit
- Macro: Introduce yourself fluently

### 5. Autonomy & Choice
- Choose your drills
- Control difficulty (pick step)
- Set your own pace
