## ‚ùå Duplicates Removed

### 1. Unit 63: "Question Words Consolidated" ‚Üí DELETED
**Reason:** Exact duplicate of Unit 34 "Question Words (Interrogatives)"
- Both units taught: o que, quem, onde, quando, como, por que, qual/quais, quanto/a/os/as
- Both covered same examples and practice formats
- **Unit 34 kept** (now Unit 38), Unit 63 removed

### 2. Unit 74: "Por vs Para (Critical Preposition Distinction)" ‚Üí DELETED
**Reason:** Exact duplicate of Unit 38 "Para vs Por (Two meanings of for)"
- Both units taught por (duration, cause, means, through, exchange) vs para (destination, purpose, recipient, deadline, intended use)
- Both provided same memory tricks and comprehensive coverage
- **Unit 38 kept** (now Unit 42), Unit 74 removed

## ‚¨ÜÔ∏è High-Frequency Content Moved Early

### Numbers Moved from 44-46 ‚Üí 15-17

**OLD POSITION:** Units 44-46 (after 43 units of instruction)
**NEW POSITION:** Units 15-17 (after basic foundation)

| New # | Old # | Unit Name |
|-------|-------|-----------|
| 15 | 44 | Numbers 0-20 (Cardinal Numbers - Foundation) |
| 16 | 45 | Numbers 21-100 (Extending the System) |
| 17 | 46 | Age Expression (Tenho X anos) |

**Pedagogical Rationale:**
- Numbers are **HIGH FREQUENCY** - needed for ages, dates, time, prices, phone numbers
- Students previously went 43 units without formal number instruction
- Now students learn numbers right after basic possessives (Unit 14)
- Enables natural age expressions, counting, quantities much earlier
- Critical foundation for later units on time (48-49), dates (50-51), shopping (65)

### Progressive Tenses Moved from 75 ‚Üí 30

**OLD POSITION:** Unit 75 (near the end of curriculum)
**NEW POSITION:** Unit 30 (immediately after Future tense)

| New # | Old # | Unit Name |
|-------|-------|-----------|
| 30 | 75 | Progressive Tenses - Estar + Gerund (Estou Fazendo) |

**Pedagogical Rationale:**
- Progressive tenses (estar + gerund) are **EXTREMELY COMMON** in Brazilian Portuguese
- Brazilians use "Estou fazendo" constantly in daily speech
- Unit 12 introduced ESTAR for location/states
- Unit 29 (old 26) teaches future with "vou + infinitive"
- **Perfect sequence:** Future (29) ‚Üí Progressive (30) ‚Üí Past tenses (34+)
- Students now learn all major present/near-future constructions before tackling past
