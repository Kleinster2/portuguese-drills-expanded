**Moved to Phase 1 (and renumbered to Q29-Q30):**

## Pattern Analysis

### Comprehension Questions (7 total)
All comprehension questions show complete Portuguese sentences with "eu":
- Q1: Eu sou professor
- Q11: Eu estou no trabalho
- Q16: Eu moro em São Paulo ✅
- Q18: Eu sempre falo português ✅
- Q20: Eu trabalho no banco todos os dias ✅
- Q22: Eu estudo português todos os dias
- Q24: Eu estou cansado

**The Trinity** (morar, falar, trabalhar) all have comprehension + production questions.

### Production Questions (25 total)
Students must actively produce the correct form by:
- Conjugating verbs (sou, estou, tenho, **moro, falo, trabalho**, estudo, fico, gosto)
- Choosing correct articles (o/a/os/as, um/uma/uns/umas)
- Using correct prepositions and contractions (do/das, no/na, de)
- Applying adjective agreement (alto/altas)

## Pedagogical Sequence

Phase 1 now follows this optimal progression:

1. **Identity** (Ser) - "I am a teacher"
2. **Origin** (de/do/da) - "I am from Brazil"
3. **Articles** - Basic noun phrases
4. **Location/State** (Estar) - "I am at home"
5. **Agreement** - Gender/number
6. **THE TRINITY** - Core self-introduction verbs ✅
   - morar (where I live)
   - falar (what I speak)
   - trabalhar (where I work)
7. **Study** (estudar) - Situational
8. **State distinction** (Ser vs Estar) - Critical distinction
9. **Possession** (Ter) - "I have two brothers"
10. **Preferences** (Gostar) - "I like music"

**Extended/Transitional:**
11. **Ficar** (Unit 21) - Less central, "where I stay"

This sequence moves from identity → location → **core actions** → possession → preferences, which is the natural order for beginner communication.

---

## The Self-Introduction Trinity ✅

After Units 7-9, students can introduce themselves with the three most universal -ar verbs:

```
Eu moro em São Paulo.        (I live in São Paulo)
Eu falo português.           (I speak Portuguese)
Eu trabalho no hospital.     (I work at the hospital)
```

These form the backbone of any self-introduction in Portuguese, which is why they now have dedicated, parallel units with clear emphasis.
