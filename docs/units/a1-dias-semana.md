## Outcomes

- Name all seven days of the week
- Ask and answer what day it is
- Say when events happen (on Monday, on Saturday)
- Use shortened forms (segunda vs segunda-feira)
- Express recurring events (every Monday, on Saturdays)
- Talk about the weekend

## Vocabulary

- **semana** (week)
- **dia/dias** (day/days)
- **Days of the week:**
  - segunda-feira (Monday - literally "second fair/market day")
  - terça-feira (Tuesday - literally "third fair day")
  - quarta-feira (Wednesday - literally "fourth fair day")
  - quinta-feira (Thursday - literally "fifth fair day")
  - sexta-feira (Friday - literally "sixth fair day")
  - sábado (Saturday)
  - domingo (Sunday)
- **fim de semana** (weekend)
- **dia útil** (weekday - literally "useful day")
- **hoje** (today)
- **amanhã** (tomorrow)
- **ontem** (yesterday)

## Grammar

- **Days are MASCULINE** (o domingo, o sábado)
- **Days with "feira"** can drop "-feira" in speech: segunda, terça, quarta, quinta, sexta
- **Preposition "em" for "on"** a specific day:
  - na segunda(-feira) (on Monday)
  - no sábado (on Saturday)
  - no domingo (on Sunday)
- **Plural for recurring days:**
  - às segundas (on Mondays - every Monday)
  - aos sábados (on Saturdays - every Saturday)
- **NO article** for "today": hoje é segunda (today is Monday) - NOT "hoje é a segunda"

### Exemplar sentences

- Hoje é sexta-feira (Today is Friday)
- Amanhã é sábado (Tomorrow is Saturday)
- Ontem foi quinta-feira (Yesterday was Thursday)
- Trabalho de segunda a sexta (I work Monday to Friday)
- Vou viajar no domingo (I'm going to travel on Sunday)
- Tenho aula na terça (I have class on Tuesday)
- Aos sábados eu acordo tarde (On Saturdays I wake up late)
- Que dia é hoje? (What day is today?)
- O fim de semana começa na sexta (The weekend starts on Friday)
