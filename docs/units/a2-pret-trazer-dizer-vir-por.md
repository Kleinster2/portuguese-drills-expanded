## Outcomes

- Talk about bringing things in the past (trouxe)
- Report what people said (disse)
- Talk about coming/arrival in the past (vim/veio)
- Talk about placing/putting things (pus/pôs)
- Combine with all other past tense verbs for richer storytelling
- Use 14 irregular past tense verbs total (Units 28, 29, 33, 35)

## Vocabulary

- **Four more essential irregular verbs in preterite:**
  - trazer (to bring)
  - dizer (to say/tell)
  - vir (to come)
  - pôr (to put/place)
- **Additional vocabulary:**
  - presente (gift/present)
  - mensagem (message)
  - notícia (news)
  - verdade (truth)
  - mentira (lie)
  - aqui (here)
  - lá (there)
  - mesa (table)
  - lugar (place)

## Grammar

- **Preterite tense for trazer, dizer, vir, and pôr**
- All four verbs are highly irregular with unique stems
- Conjugation patterns:
  - **trazer**: trouxe, trouxe, trouxemos, trouxeram
  - **dizer**: disse, disse, dissemos, disseram
  - **vir**: vim, veio, viemos, vieram
  - **pôr**: pus, pôs, pusemos, puseram

### Exemplar sentences

- Trouxe um presente para você (I brought a gift for you)
- Ela trouxe a comida (She brought the food)
- Ele disse que vai viajar (He said that he's going to travel)
- Dissemos a verdade (We told the truth)
- Vim de ônibus (I came by bus)
- Ele veio aqui ontem (He came here yesterday)
- Pus o livro na mesa (I put the book on the table)
- Ela pôs as chaves lá (She put the keys there)
