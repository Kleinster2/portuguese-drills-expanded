## Outcomes

- Talk about what they did/made (fiz)
- Talk about what they saw/watched (vi)
- Talk about what they gave (dei)
- Answer "O que você fez?" (What did you do?) - one of the most common questions
- Combine with Units 26-28 for rich storytelling

## Vocabulary

- Vocabulary that pairs with these verbs:
  - perguntas (questions)
  - trabalho (work/task)
  - exercícios (exercises)
  - presente (gift/present)
  - dinheiro (money)
  - ajuda (help)

## Grammar

- **Three more essential irregular verbs in preterite**
- **fazer (to make/do)**: fiz, fez, fizemos, fizeram
- **ver (to see/watch)**: vi, viu, vimos, viram
- **dar (to give)**: dei, deu, demos, deram
- These verbs are extremely common in past tense conversations
- Each has its own irregular pattern - must be memorized

### Exemplar sentences

- Fiz exercícios de manhã (I did exercises in the morning)
- Fiz o trabalho ontem (I did the work yesterday)
- Fiz uma pergunta (I asked a question)
- Vi meus amigos (I saw my friends)
- Vi um filme ontem (I saw/watched a movie yesterday)
- Não vi nada (I didn't see anything)
- Dei um presente para ela (I gave her a gift)
- Dei dinheiro (I gave money)
- Ele me deu ajuda (He gave me help)
