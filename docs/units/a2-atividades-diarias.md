## Outcomes

- Name 17 daily activity verbs
- Conjugate regular daily routine verbs in present tense
- Use irregular DORMIR correctly
- Describe their daily routine with times
- Sequence activities in order

## Vocabulary

- **Daily routine verbs:**
  - acordar (to wake up)
  - levantar (to get up)
  - tomar banho (to take a shower/bath)
  - escovar (to brush)
  - pentear (to comb)
  - vestir (to dress/get dressed)
  - tomar café (to have breakfast - literally "take coffee")
  - almoçar (to have lunch)
  - jantar (to have dinner)
  - estudar (to study)
  - trabalhar (to work) [review]
  - descansar (to rest)
  - assistir (to watch)
  - dormir (to sleep)
  - cozinhar (to cook)
  - limpar (to clean)
  - lavar (to wash)

## Grammar

- **All verbs are regular except DORMIR:**
  - **DORMIR (to sleep)** is irregular (o → ue stem change in some forms):
    - eu durmo (I sleep)
    - você dorme (you sleep)
    - ele/ela dorme (he/she sleeps)
    - nós dormimos (we sleep)
    - vocês dormem (you all sleep)
    - eles/elas dormem (they sleep)

- **Reflexive use with daily routine:**
  - Some activities can be reflexive (doing to yourself):
  - **vestir-se** (to get dressed - literally "dress oneself")
  - **pentear-se** (to comb one's hair)
  - **levantar-se** (to get up - literally "lift oneself")
  - More common in formal speech; often dropped in casual Brazilian Portuguese

- **Time expressions with daily activities:**
  - Eu acordo **às** sete horas. (I wake up at 7 o'clock.)
  - Eu almoço **ao meio-dia**. (I have lunch at noon.)
  - Eu durmo **à** noite. (I sleep at night.)
  - Use **às** (at) with specific times

### Exemplar sentences

- Eu acordo às sete da manhã. (I wake up at 7 AM.)
- Eu tomo banho todos os dias. (I shower every day.)
- Ela escova os dentes. (She brushes her teeth.)
- Nós almoçamos ao meio-dia. (We have lunch at noon.)
- Eu trabalho de segunda a sexta. (I work Monday to Friday.)
- Ele dorme oito horas por noite. (He sleeps eight hours per night.)
- Eu cozinho o jantar. (I cook dinner.)
