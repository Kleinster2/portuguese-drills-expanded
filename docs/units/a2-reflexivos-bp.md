## Outcomes

- Introduce themselves formally: "Me chamo [name]"
- Talk about daily routine (getting up, sitting down, going to bed)
- Express how they feel (me sinto bem/mal/cansado/feliz)
- Use reflexive verbs in all three tenses (present, future, past)
- Understand difference between reflexive and non-reflexive verbs
- Ask someone's name formally: "Como você se chama?"

## Vocabulary

- **Reflexive pronouns:**
  - **me** (myself)
  - **se** (yourself/himself/herself/themselves)
  - **nos** (ourselves)
- **Common reflexive verbs:**
  - chamar-se (to call oneself / to be named)
  - levantar-se (to get up)
  - sentar-se (to sit down)
  - sentir-se (to feel)
  - vestir-se (to get dressed)
  - deitar-se (to lie down / go to bed)
  - lembrar-se (to remember)
  - esquecer-se (to forget)
- **Daily routine vocabulary:**
  - de manhã (in the morning)
  - à tarde (in the afternoon)
  - à noite (at night)
  - cedo (early)
  - tarde (late)
  - bem (well)
  - mal (badly)
  - cansado/a (tired)
  - feliz (happy)

## Grammar

- **Reflexive verbs: action reflects back on the subject**
- **Reflexive pronouns in Brazilian Portuguese go BEFORE the conjugated verb**
- Pattern: subject + reflexive pronoun + conjugated verb
  - Eu **me** chamo Maria (I call myself Maria = My name is Maria)
  - Eu **me** levanto cedo (I get up early)
  - Eu **me** sinto bem (I feel well)
- Reflexive pronouns for each subject:
  - eu → **me** (myself)
  - você/ele/ela → **se** (yourself/himself/herself)
  - nós → **nos** (ourselves)
  - vocês/eles/elas → **se** (yourselves/themselves)
- Common use: **"Como você se chama?"** = "What's your name?" (literally: How do you call yourself?)
- Answer: **"Me chamo [name]"** or informal **"Meu nome é [name]"** (Unit 13)

### Exemplar sentences

- Me chamo Maria (My name is Maria)
- Me levanto às 7h (I get up at 7am)
- Me sinto bem (I feel well)
- Me sinto cansado (I feel tired)
- Não me sinto bem (I don't feel well)
- Me visto rápido de manhã (I get dressed quickly in the morning)
- Me sento aqui (I sit here)
- Me deito tarde (I go to bed late)
- Você se lembra? (Do you remember?)
- Não me lembro (I don't remember)
- Como você se chama? (What's your name?)
