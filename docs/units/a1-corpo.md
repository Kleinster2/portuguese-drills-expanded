## Outcomes

- Name 14 essential body parts
- Use correct irregular plurals (mãos, pés)
- Describe pain or problems with body parts
- Use the verb DOER correctly with body parts
- Describe physical features (hair color, eye color)

## Vocabulary

- **Basic body parts:**
  - a cabeça (head)
  - o cabelo (hair)
  - o olho (eye)
  - a orelha (ear)
  - o nariz (nose)
  - a boca (mouth)
  - o dente (tooth)
  - o braço (arm)
  - a mão (hand)
  - o dedo (finger/toe)
  - a perna (leg)
  - o pé (foot)
  - a barriga (belly/stomach)
  - as costas (back - always plural)

## Grammar

- **Plural forms with body parts:**
  - **os olhos** (eyes - plural)
  - **as mãos** (hands - irregular plural! not "maos")
  - **os pés** (feet - irregular plural! not "pes")
  - **as costas** (back - ALWAYS plural in Portuguese)

- **Describing pain/problems with body parts:**
  - Use DOER (to hurt) - special verb:
    - "**Meu** braço **dói**" (My arm hurts)
    - "**Minha** cabeça **dói**" (My head hurts)
    - "**Meus** olhos **doem**" (My eyes hurt - plural verb!)
  - Structure: possessive + body part + doer (conjugated)

- **The verb DOER (to hurt):**
  - 3rd person ONLY (body part is the subject doing the hurting)
  - **Singular:** dói (one thing hurts)
  - **Plural:** doem (multiple things hurt)
  - Examples:
    - O pé dói. (The foot hurts.)
    - Os pés doem. (The feet hurt.)

### Exemplar sentences

- Minha cabeça dói. (My head hurts.)
- Meus olhos são azuis. (My eyes are blue.)
- Meu cabelo é preto. (My hair is black.)
- Meus pés doem. (My feet hurt.)
- Eu tenho dor de barriga. (I have a stomachache.)
- Minhas mãos são pequenas. (My hands are small.)
- O braço dele dói. (His arm hurts.)
