## Outcomes

- Express future plans and intentions
- Talk about what they're going to do (later today, tomorrow, next week, etc.)
- Use the full conjugation of "ir" + infinitive for all pronouns
- Apply time expressions to indicate when
- Distinguish between present actions (Unit 23: "Estudo") and future plans (Unit 24: "Vou estudar")

## Vocabulary

- amanhã (tomorrow)
- depois (later, after)
- hoje à noite (tonight)
- no fim de semana (on the weekend)
- na próxima semana (next week)
- no próximo mês (next month)
- Time expressions already known: agora (now), hoje (today), cedo (early), tarde (late)

## Grammar

- **Future construction: vou + infinitive** = "going to" (immediate/planned future)
- This combines two concepts students already know:
  - **vou** from Unit 8 (irregular verb "ir")
  - **infinitives** from Unit 19 (gosto de + infinitive)
- Pattern: subject + vou/vai/vamos/vão + infinitive verb
- Expressing future plans and intentions
- Time expressions to indicate when actions will happen

### Exemplar sentences

- Vou estudar português amanhã (I'm going to study Portuguese tomorrow)
- Vou trabalhar hoje à noite (I'm going to work tonight)
- Vou viajar no fim de semana (I'm going to travel on the weekend)
- Vou comer no restaurante depois (I'm going to eat at the restaurant later)
- Não vou assistir filmes hoje (I'm not going to watch movies today)
- Vou dormir cedo (I'm going to sleep early)
