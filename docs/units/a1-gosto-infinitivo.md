## Outcomes

- Express what activities they like/dislike
- Use infinitive forms (no need to conjugate!)
- Expand vocabulary massively (can use any verb as infinitive)
- Combine multiple activities with "e"
- Apply negation to activities

## Vocabulary

- Verb infinitives as activities:
  - estudar (to study)
  - viajar (to travel)
  - cozinhar (to cook)
  - cantar (to sing)
  - dançar (to dance)
  - correr (to run)
  - nadar (to swim)
  - ler (to read)
  - escrever (to write)
  - assistir (to watch)
  - ouvir (to listen)
  - dormir (to sleep)
  - acordar (to wake up)

## Grammar

- Expanding "gosto de + infinitive" (introduced in Unit 18)
- Infinitive verbs as objects (no conjugation needed!)
- Students can now use verbs they haven't learned to conjugate yet
- Combining multiple activities: "Gosto de estudar e viajar"
- Negation with activities: "Não gosto de acordar cedo"

### Exemplar sentences

- Gosto de estudar português
- Gosto muito de viajar
- Não gosto de acordar cedo
- Gosto de cozinhar em casa
- Gosto de correr no parque
- Gosto de dançar e cantar
- Não gosto de nadar
- Gosto de ler livros
- Gosto de assistir filmes
