## Outcomes

- Distinguish "em casa" (my home) from "na casa de" (someone else's home)
- Express whose house they're at using correct contractions
- Combine multiple contraction rules naturally
- Use possessive "de" with people's names
- Master the triple contraction pattern

## Vocabulary

- casa de (house of)
- People's names: João, Maria, Pedro, Ana
- Family members: pai (father), mãe (mother), irmão (brother), irmã (sister), amigo/amiga (friend)
- Revisit: de + articles = do/da/dos/das

## Grammar

- Fixed expression: "em casa" (at home - NO article) = at MY home
- Location at someone else's house: "na casa de [person]"
- Triple contraction: **na casa do/da/dos/das**
- Combining contractions:
  - em + a = na (from Unit 3)
  - de + o/a/os/as = do/da/dos/das (from Unit 2)
  - Together: na casa + de + article = **na casa do/da/dos/das**
- Possessive with "de": na casa do João (João's house), na casa da Maria (Maria's house)

### Exemplar sentences

- Estou em casa (at my home)
- Estou na casa do João (at João's house)
- Estou na casa da Maria (at Maria's house)
- Estou na casa do Pedro (at Pedro's house)
- Estou na casa da Ana (at Ana's house)
- Vou à casa do meu amigo (I'm going to my friend's house)
- Ele está na casa da irmã (He's at his sister's house)
