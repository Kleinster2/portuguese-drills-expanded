## Outcomes

- Describe common health problems and symptoms
- Use ESTAR correctly for being sick
- Use TER for specific illnesses/symptoms
- Name medical places and professionals
- Ask for medicine and describe problems to doctor

## Vocabulary

- **Health conditions:**
  - doente (sick)
  - saudável (healthy)
  - a febre (fever)
  - a tosse (cough)
  - o resfriado (cold/flu)
  - a gripe (flu)
  - a dor (pain)
  - o remédio (medicine)
  - o comprimido (pill)
  - a receita (prescription)

- **Medical places:**
  - o hospital (hospital)
  - a clínica (clinic)
  - o consultório (doctor's office)
  - o posto de saúde (health center)
  - a farmácia (pharmacy)

- **Medical professionals (review + expand):**
  - o médico / a médica (doctor)
  - o enfermeiro / a enfermeira (nurse)
  - o dentista / a dentista (dentist)

## Grammar

- **ESTAR doente (to be sick) - uses ESTAR:**
  - Eu **estou** doente. (I am sick - temporary condition)
  - NOT "sou doente" - that would mean permanently sick!
  - Temporary health states always use ESTAR

- **TER (to have) with illnesses:**
  - Eu **tenho** febre. (I have a fever.)
  - Eu **tenho** tosse. (I have a cough.)
  - Eu **tenho** gripe. (I have the flu.)
  - Use TER (not ESTAR) for specific symptoms/illnesses

- **ESTAR COM (to be with) - alternative structure:**
  - Eu **estou com** febre. (I am with fever = I have a fever.)
  - Eu **estou com** dor de cabeça. (I have a headache.)
  - Both TER and ESTAR COM are correct!

- **Illness expressions:**
  - estar doente (to be sick)
  - estar bem (to be well)
  - estar mal (to feel bad/unwell)
  - ficar doente (to get/become sick)
  - melhorar (to get better)
  - piorar (to get worse)

### Exemplar sentences

- Eu estou doente. (I am sick.)
- Eu tenho febre. (I have a fever.)
- Eu estou com dor de cabeça. (I have a headache.)
- Eu preciso de um médico. (I need a doctor.)
- Eu vou à farmácia. (I'm going to the pharmacy.)
- Ela está com gripe. (She has the flu.)
- Eu preciso de remédio. (I need medicine.)
