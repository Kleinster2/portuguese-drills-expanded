## Outcomes

- Name 21 shopping and money terms
- Use CUSTAR correctly (3rd person only)
- Ask and understand prices in reais
- Talk about payment methods (Pix, card, cash)
- Describe shopping experiences

## Vocabulary

- **Money:**
  - o dinheiro (money)
  - o real (Brazilian currency - singular)
  - os reais (Brazilian currency - plural)
  - a moeda (coin)
  - a nota (bill/banknote)
  - o cartão (card)
  - o cartão de crédito (credit card)
  - o cartão de débito (debit card)
  - o Pix (instant payment system)
  - o troco (change)

- **Shopping:**
  - a loja (store)
  - o mercado (market)
  - o supermercado (supermarket)
  - a farmácia (pharmacy)
  - comprar (to buy)
  - vender (to sell)
  - custar (to cost)
  - pagar (to pay)
  - o preço (price)
  - barato (cheap)
  - caro (expensive)

## Grammar

- **Brazilian currency: REAL/REAIS**
  - **Singular:** um real (R$ 1,00)
  - **Plural:** dois reais, três reais, cem reais
  - Written: R$ (símbolo do real)
  - R$ 10,00 = dez reais

- **CUSTAR (to cost) - special verb:**
  - **3rd person only** (like DOER in Unit 57)
  - Subject is the thing that costs, not the person!
  - **Singular:** custa (it costs)
  - **Plural:** custam (they cost)
  - Examples:
    - Quanto **custa**? (How much does it cost?)
    - **Custa** dez reais. (It costs ten reais.)
    - Os livros **custam** vinte reais. (The books cost twenty reais.)

- **PAGAR (to pay) - takes direct object OR por:**
  - Eu pago dez reais. (I pay ten reais.) - direct object
  - Eu pago **pelo** livro. (I pay for the book.) - por + o = pelo
  - Both structures are correct

- **PIX - Brazilian instant payment:**
  - Revolutionary payment system in Brazil (launched 2020)
  - Instant bank-to-bank transfer via phone
  - "Você aceita Pix?" (Do you accept Pix?)
  - "Eu faço um Pix" (I'll make a Pix payment)
  - Has largely replaced cash in Brazil!

### Exemplar sentences

- Quanto custa? (How much does it cost?)
- Custa dez reais. (It costs ten reais.)
- Eu pago com cartão. (I pay with card.)
- Você aceita Pix? (Do you accept Pix?)
- Isso é muito caro. (This is very expensive.)
- Eu compro no supermercado. (I buy at the supermarket.)
- Eu preciso de troco. (I need change.)
