## Outcomes

- Name 24+ common furniture and household items
- Use TER to describe what's in rooms
- Describe where furniture is located
- Use adjectives with furniture
- Describe their home in detail

## Vocabulary

- **Living room furniture:**
  - o sofá (sofa/couch)
  - a poltrona (armchair)
  - a mesa de centro (coffee table)
  - a estante (bookshelf)
  - a televisão (television)
  - o tapete (rug/carpet)

- **Bedroom furniture:**
  - a cama (bed)
  - o guarda-roupa (wardrobe/closet)
  - o travesseiro (pillow)
  - o cobertor (blanket)
  - o lençol (sheet)

- **Kitchen items:**
  - a geladeira (refrigerator)
  - o fogão (stove)
  - o micro-ondas (microwave)
  - a pia (sink)
  - a mesa (table)
  - a cadeira (chair)

- **Bathroom items:**
  - o chuveiro (shower)
  - a pia (sink)
  - o vaso sanitário (toilet)
  - o espelho (mirror)
  - a toalha (towel)

## Grammar

- **TER (to have) with furniture:**
  - Na sala **tem** um sofá. (In the living room there is a sofa.)
  - Meu quarto **tem** uma cama grande. (My room has a big bed.)
  - Using TER for "there is/there are" in rooms

- **Prepositions with furniture:**
  - **em cima da** mesa (on top of the table)
  - **embaixo da** cama (under the bed)
  - **ao lado do** sofá (next to the sofa)
  - **na** estante (in/on the bookshelf)

- **Describing furniture:**
  - sofá grande (big sofa)
  - cama confortável (comfortable bed)
  - mesa pequena (small table)
  - Adjectives agree with furniture gender/number

### Exemplar sentences

- Na sala tem um sofá. (In the living room there's a sofa.)
- Meu quarto tem uma cama grande. (My room has a big bed.)
- A geladeira está na cozinha. (The refrigerator is in the kitchen.)
- O livro está em cima da mesa. (The book is on top of the table.)
- Eu preciso de uma cadeira. (I need a chair.)
- O espelho está no banheiro. (The mirror is in the bathroom.)
- Meu sofá é muito confortável. (My sofa is very comfortable.)
