## Outcomes

- Express how they get around
- Use fourth -AR verb (reinforcing the -o pattern)
- Distinguish "andar de" + transport vs "andar a pé"
- Talk about daily transportation

## Vocabulary

- ando (I walk/ride) - fourth regular -AR verb
- de (by/of) - used with transport
- a pé (on foot) - fixed expression
- Example transports:
  - ônibus (bus)
  - bicicleta (bicycle)
  - carro (car)

## Grammar

- Fourth regular -AR verb: ando (I walk/ride)
- "Ando de" + transport = ride/go by [transport]
- "Ando a pé" = walk (on foot) - fixed expression
- No articles with transport in "ando de" construction

### Exemplar sentences

- Eu ando de ônibus
- Eu ando de bicicleta
- Eu ando de carro
- Eu ando a pé
- Eu ando de ônibus todo dia (I take the bus every day)
