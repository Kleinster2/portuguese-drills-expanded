## Outcomes

- Name 11 essential clothing items
- Use correct gender for clothing vocabulary
- Describe what they're wearing with USAR
- Combine clothing with colors from Unit 55
- Ask what others are wearing

## Vocabulary

- **Essential clothing items:**
  - a camisa (shirt - feminine!)
  - a camiseta (t-shirt - feminine)
  - a calça (pants - singular in Portuguese!)
  - a saia (skirt)
  - o vestido (dress)
  - o casaco (jacket/coat)
  - o sapato (shoe)
  - a meia (sock)
  - o chapéu (hat)
  - os óculos (glasses - always plural)
  - a roupa (clothing - singular means "clothes")

## Grammar

- **Gender surprises in clothing:**
  - **camisa is FEMININE** even though it often refers to men's shirts
  - **vestido is MASCULINE** even though dresses are typically worn by women
  - Gender is about the word, not who wears it!

- **Singular/Plural differences from English:**
  - **calça** (pants) is SINGULAR in Portuguese:
    - ✅ "Eu uso **uma** calça" (I'm wearing pants)
    - ❌ "Eu uso calças" (sounds like multiple pairs)
  - **óculos** (glasses) is PLURAL in both languages:
    - "Eu uso óculos" (I wear glasses)

- **Wearing/Using:** USAR (to wear/use)
  - Eu uso uma camisa. (I'm wearing a shirt.)
  - Você usa óculos? (Do you wear glasses?)
  - Ela usa vestido. (She's wearing a dress.)

- **Verbs for getting dressed:**
  - **vestir** (to dress/wear): Eu visto uma camisa. (I put on/wear a shirt.)
  - **usar** (to use/wear): Eu uso sapatos. (I wear shoes.)
  - More common: usar (simpler, more versatile)

### Exemplar sentences

- Eu uso uma camisa branca. (I'm wearing a white shirt.)
- Ela usa um vestido vermelho. (She's wearing a red dress.)
- Você usa óculos? (Do you wear glasses?)
- Meu sapato é preto. (My shoe is black.)
- Ele usa uma calça azul. (He's wearing blue pants.)
- Eu preciso de um casaco. (I need a jacket.)
- A saia é verde. (The skirt is green.)
