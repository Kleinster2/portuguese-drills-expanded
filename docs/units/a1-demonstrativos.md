## Outcomes

- Use three-way demonstrative system correctly
- Choose este/esse/aquele based on distance
- Make demonstratives agree in gender and number
- Use demonstrative pronouns (este, aquele)
- Use neuter demonstratives (isto, isso, aquilo)

## Vocabulary

- **Demonstrative adjectives/pronouns:**
  - este/esta/estes/estas (this/these - near speaker)
  - esse/essa/esses/essas (that/those - near listener)
  - aquele/aquela/aqueles/aquelas (that/those - far from both)

- **Demonstrative pronouns (neuter):**
  - isto (this - abstract/general)
  - isso (that - abstract/general)
  - aquilo (that - abstract/general, far)

## Grammar

- **Three-way distinction in Portuguese:**

  Portuguese has THREE demonstratives, not two like English!

  **ESTE (this) - near the speaker:**
  - **Este** livro (this book - in my hand)
  - Near me, the person speaking
  - Equivalent to "this" in English

  **ESSE (that) - near the listener:**
  - **Esse** livro (that book - in your hand)
  - Near you, the person listening
  - Equivalent to "that" in English (near)

  **AQUELE (that) - far from both:**
  - **Aquele** livro (that book - over there)
  - Far from both speaker and listener
  - Equivalent to "that over there" in English

- **Gender and number agreement:**

  Demonstratives MUST agree with the noun:

  **Masculine singular:**
  - este livro (this book)
  - esse carro (that car)
  - aquele homem (that man over there)

  **Feminine singular:**
  - esta casa (this house)
  - essa mulher (that woman)
  - aquela loja (that store over there)

  **Masculine plural:**
  - estes livros (these books)
  - esses carros (those cars)
  - aqueles homens (those men over there)

  **Feminine plural:**
  - estas casas (these houses)
  - essas mulheres (those women)
  - aquelas lojas (those stores over there)

- **Demonstrative pronouns (replacing nouns):**
  - Eu quero **este**. (I want this one.)
  - Eu prefiro **aquele**. (I prefer that one over there.)
  - **Estas** são minhas. (These are mine.)

- **Neuter demonstratives (for abstract things):**
  - **Isto** é importante. (This is important.)
  - **Isso** é verdade. (That is true.)
  - **Aquilo** é estranho. (That is strange.)
  - Use when NOT referring to specific noun

### Exemplar sentences

- Este livro é meu. (This book is mine - I have it)
- Esse livro é seu? (Is that book yours? - you have it)
- Aquele livro é caro. (That book is expensive - over there)
- Eu quero esta camisa. (I want this shirt.)
- Você gosta desses sapatos? (Do you like those shoes? - pointing to listener)
- Aquelas casas são grandes. (Those houses are big - over there)
- Isto é importante. (This is important.)
