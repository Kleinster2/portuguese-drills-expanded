## Outcomes

- Give a complete address (city + street)
- Understand that street/avenue names don't take articles
- Provide increasingly specific location information

## Vocabulary

- rua (street) - feminine
- avenida (avenue) - feminine
- Street names: Paulista, Ipiranga, Copacabana, etc.

## Grammar

- Specific address with "na" (em + a = na)
- Both "rua" and "avenida" are feminine → always "na"
- No article before street names (na Rua Paulista, not na Rua da Paulista)

### Exemplar sentences

- Eu moro na Rua Paulista
- Eu moro na Avenida Copacabana
- Eu moro na Rua Augusta
- Eu moro em São Paulo, na Rua Consolação
