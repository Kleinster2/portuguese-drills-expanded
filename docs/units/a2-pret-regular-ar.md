## Outcomes

- Talk about completed past actions with regular -AR verbs
- Use all -AR verbs learned so far in past tense
- Distinguish present from past using time expressions
- Notice that nós form is SAME in present and past (context determines meaning)
- Ask questions about past events

## Vocabulary

- **ontem** (yesterday)
- **na semana passada** (last week)
- **no mês passado** (last month)
- **no ano passado** (last year)
- **anteontem** (the day before yesterday)
- **há [time]** (ago) - há dois dias (two days ago)

## Grammar

- **Preterite tense for regular -AR verbs** (simple past - completed actions)
- Past tense endings for -AR verbs:
  - eu: -**ei** (morei, trabalhei, estudei)
  - você/ele/ela: -**ou** (morou, trabalhou, estudou)
  - nós: -**amos** (moramos, trabalhamos, estudamos) - **SAME as present!**
  - vocês/eles/elas: -**aram** (moraram, trabalharam, estudaram)
- Used for actions that happened at a specific time in the past and are complete
- Time expressions that signal past tense

### Exemplar sentences

- Morei no Brasil (I lived in Brazil)
- Trabalhei ontem (I worked yesterday)
- Estudei português na semana passada (I studied Portuguese last week)
- Ele morou em São Paulo (He lived in São Paulo)
- Nós trabalhamos ontem (We worked yesterday - same as present!)
- Eles falaram português (They spoke Portuguese)
- Você gostou do filme? (Did you like the movie?)
- Não gostei (I didn't like it)
