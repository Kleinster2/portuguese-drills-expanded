## Outcomes

After this unit, the student can:

- Form the futuro composto do indicativo (terei/terás/terá/teremos/terão + particípio) — the **future perfect**
- Express an action that **will be completed before** another future point or action
- Recognize that this form is mostly written/formal in spoken BP, frequently replaced by *vou ter feito* in conversation

## Vocabulary

- **terei / terás / terá / teremos / terão + particípio** — auxiliary + past participle
- Common temporal anchors: *até* (by [time]), *antes de* (before), *quando* (when, anchoring future)

## Grammar

Form: futuro do indicativo of *ter* + particípio passado.

| Pessoa | Auxiliar | + particípio |
|---|---|---|
| eu | terei | falado |
| você / ele / ela | terá | falado |
| nós | teremos | falado |
| vocês / eles / elas | terão | falado |

The form expresses an action **completed before** a future reference point. The reference point may be explicit (*até o fim do ano*) or implicit (*quando você chegar*).

### Exemplar sentences

- *Até o fim do ano, eu **terei publicado** o livro.* — By the end of the year, I'll have published the book.
- *Quando você chegar, eu já **terei terminado**.* — When you arrive, I'll have already finished.
- *Em 2030, ela **terá se aposentado**.* — In 2030, she'll have retired.
- *Antes do casamento, eles **terão se mudado** para a nova casa.* — Before the wedding, they'll have moved to the new house.
- *Você **terá visto** o filme antes de eu chegar?* — Will you have seen the movie before I arrive?
- *Nós **teremos discutido** todos os pontos até o final da reunião.* — We'll have discussed all the points by the end of the meeting.

### Spoken vs written register

In spoken BP, this form is uncommon. The natural alternatives are:

| Register | Form | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Formal / written | futuro composto | *Terei terminado até sexta-feira.* |
| Spoken / informal | *vou ter* + particípio | *Vou ter terminado até sexta-feira.* |
| Spoken / informal | simple future + anchor | *Termino até sexta-feira.* |

EP usage is similar but slightly more comfortable with the synthetic form in writing.

## Traps

- **Don't confuse with futuro próximo (vou fazer).** *Vou fazer* = will do (intent). *Terei feito* = will have done (completion before a future point). Different aspects entirely.
- **The form is rare in spoken BP.** Don't drill it as the default future expression — it's a register-specific form for written/formal contexts. For spoken BP, *vou ter feito* is the natural compound future.
- **Particípios irregulares apply.** *Terei dito, terei feito, terei posto* — same set as the other compound tenses.
- **Cluster 4 form unit; downstream from b2-futuro-composto-subj.** The indicative future-perfect appears in main clauses; the subjunctive future-perfect appears after temporal conjunctions (*quando, assim que, depois que*) — see `b2-futuro-composto-subj`.
