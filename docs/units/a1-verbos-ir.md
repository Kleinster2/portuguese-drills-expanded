## Outcomes

- Conjugate -IR verbs in present tense
- Recognize all three regular verb patterns (-AR, -ER, -IR)
- See that "-o" is truly universal for "I" across all verb types
- Apply negation to -IR verbs
- Connect back to Unit 16: "Gosto de assistir" + "Assisto filmes"

## Vocabulary

- abro (I open) - first -IR verb
- parto (I leave/depart) - second -IR verb
- assisto (I watch) - third -IR verb (infinitive already known from Unit 19)
- Common vocabulary:
  - a porta (the door)
  - a janela (the window)
  - a loja (the store)
  - cedo (early)
  - tarde (late)
  - filmes (movies/films)
  - séries (TV series)

## Grammar

- **-IR verb pattern: -IR → -o** (same ending as -AR and -ER verbs!)
- abrir → abro
- partir → parto
- assistir → assisto
- **REVELATION: "-o = I" works for ALL THREE verb types!**
- Complete regular verb system: -AR, -ER, -IR all use -o
- Negation with -IR verbs: não abro, não parto, não assisto

### Exemplar sentences

- Abro a porta (I open the door)
- Abro a janela de manhã (I open the window in the morning)
- Parto cedo (I leave early)
- Não parto tarde (I don't leave late)
- Assisto filmes brasileiros (I watch Brazilian films)
- Assisto séries em português (I watch series in Portuguese)
- Não assisto televisão (I don't watch television)
