## Outcomes

After this unit, the student can:

- Conjugate any verb derived from **ter** by inheriting the parent's irregular paradigm
- Apply the **3sg / 3pl accent rule** that affects all ter-derivatives in the present indicative (acute on 3sg, circumflex on 3pl)
- Use the most frequent derivatives in context (*manter, conter, obter, deter*)

## Vocabulary

The ter family — derivatives are formed by attaching a Latinate prefix to the parent verb. All inherit ter's full irregular paradigm.

| Verb | Meaning | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| **ter** | to have | foundational |
| **manter** | to maintain, keep | high |
| **conter** | to contain, hold | high |
| **obter** | to obtain, get | high |
| **deter** | to detain, stop, hold | medium — formal/news |
| **reter** | to retain, hold back | medium |
| **entreter** | to entertain | medium |
| **abster-se** | to abstain (always reflexive) | medium — voting/dietary contexts |
| **deter-se** | to stop oneself, pause | medium — reflexive |

## Grammar

The inheritance rule is total: every derivative conjugates exactly like ter. **The critical pattern to memorize is the 3sg/3pl accent shift in the present indicative**, which appears in every derivative and is the highest-error point.

### Ter paradigm (parent — must know cold)

| Tense | eu | tu | você/ele/ela | nós | vocês/eles/elas |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Presente | tenho | tens | tem | temos | têm |
| Pretérito | tive | tiveste | teve | tivemos | tiveram |
| Imperfeito | tinha | tinhas | tinha | tínhamos | tinham |
| Futuro | terei | terás | terá | teremos | terão |
| Condicional | teria | terias | teria | teríamos | teriam |
| Pres. subj. | tenha | tenhas | tenha | tenhamos | tenham |
| Imp. subj. | tivesse | tivesses | tivesse | tivéssemos | tivessem |
| Particípio | tido | | | | |

### Derivative inheritance — example: manter

Just attach the prefix *man-* to every form, with **accent shift in 3sg/3pl present**:

| Tense | eu form | 3sg form | 3pl form |
|---|---|---|---|
| Presente | mantenho | **mantém** | **mantêm** |
| Pretérito | mantive | manteve | mantiveram |
| Imperfeito | mantinha | mantinha | mantinham |
| Futuro | manterei | manterá | manterão |
| Pres. subj. | mantenha | mantenha | mantenham |
| Imp. subj. | mantivesse | mantivesse | mantivessem |
| Particípio | mantido | | |

### The 3sg / 3pl accent shift in present

When ter takes a prefix, the stress of the 3sg form shifts to the prefixed-final syllable, and an **acute accent** marks it. The 3pl form takes a **circumflex accent**:

| Verb | 3sg present (acute) | 3pl present (circumflex) |
|---|---|---|
| ter | tem (no accent — monosyllabic) | têm (circumflex) |
| conter | **contém** | **contêm** |
| manter | **mantém** | **mantêm** |
| obter | **obtém** | **obtêm** |
| reter | **retém** | **retêm** |
| deter | **detém** | **detêm** |
| entreter | **entretém** | **entretêm** |

This accent pattern is the **single highest-error point** for ter-derivatives.

### Exemplar sentences

- *A caixa **contém** vinte livros.* — The box contains twenty books.
- *Os museus **contêm** muitas obras raras.* — The museums contain many rare works.
- *Eles **mantiveram** a casa limpa durante a viagem.* — They kept the house clean during the trip.
- *Nós **obtivemos** os resultados ontem.* — We got the results yesterday.
- *A polícia **deteve** o suspeito.* — The police detained the suspect.
- *Por favor, **retenha** os documentos até segunda-feira.* — Please hold the documents until Monday.
- *Ele **se absteve** de votar.* — He abstained from voting.

## Traps

- **Forgetting the 3sg acute / 3pl circumflex accent in present is THE big mistake.** *Contem*, *mantem*, *obtem* without the accent are wrong; the acute on 3sg and circumflex on 3pl are mandatory. Worksheets and drills must check this explicitly.
- **Don't regularize the eu-preterite.** *Mantive* (not *manteu*), *contive* (not *contei*), *obtive* (not *obtei*). Same tive/tiveste/teve paradigm as the parent.
- **Abster-se is always reflexive.** No non-reflexive *abster*. *Eu me abstenho de votar* (BP) / *Eu abstenho-me de votar* (EP). The se-pronoun is mandatory.
- **Deter has two meanings.** *Deter* = to detain (police context, *a polícia deteve*); *deter-se* (reflexive) = to stop oneself, pause. Different syntax.
- **Compound ter (auxiliary in compound tenses)** is the parent ter, not a derivative: *Tenho falado, tinha visto, terei feito*. Don't confuse compound auxiliary use with ter-derivative use.
- **Particípio is *tido*, not *tenido*.** Compounds: *mantido, contido, obtido, detido, retido, entretido, abstido*.
