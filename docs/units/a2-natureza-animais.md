## Outcomes

- Name 10+ common animals
- Name 14+ nature vocabulary items
- Talk about pets using TER
- Describe natural environments
- Use nature vocabulary with prepositions

## Vocabulary

- **Common animals:**
  - o cachorro (dog)
  - o gato (cat)
  - o pássaro (bird)
  - o peixe (fish)
  - o cavalo (horse)
  - a vaca (cow)
  - o porco (pig)
  - a galinha (chicken)
  - o coelho (rabbit)
  - o rato (rat/mouse)

- **Nature vocabulary:**
  - a árvore (tree)
  - a flor (flower)
  - a planta (plant)
  - a grama (grass)
  - a montanha (mountain)
  - o rio (river)
  - o lago (lake)
  - o mar (sea)
  - a praia (beach)
  - a floresta (forest)
  - o céu (sky)
  - o sol (sun)
  - a lua (moon)
  - a estrela (star)

## Grammar

- **TER (to have) with pets:**
  - Eu **tenho** um cachorro. (I have a dog.)
  - Você **tem** animais? (Do you have animals/pets?)
  - Structure: TER + animal

- **Animals with gender variations:**
  - **o cachorro / a cachorra** (male dog / female dog)
  - **o gato / a gata** (male cat / female cat)
  - But often use masculine for generic: "Eu tenho um cachorro" (I have a dog - could be male or female)

- **Nature with prepositions:**
  - **na** praia (at/on the beach)
  - **no** rio (in the river)
  - **na** montanha (in/on the mountain)
  - **na** floresta (in the forest)

- **Describing animals:**
  - cachorro grande (big dog)
  - gato preto (black cat)
  - pássaro pequeno (small bird)
  - Adjectives agree with animal gender

### Exemplar sentences

- Eu tenho um cachorro. (I have a dog.)
- Meu gato é preto. (My cat is black.)
- O pássaro está na árvore. (The bird is in the tree.)
- Eu gosto de ir à praia. (I like to go to the beach.)
- A montanha é alta. (The mountain is tall.)
- Tem muitas flores no jardim. (There are many flowers in the garden.)
- O rio é grande. (The river is big.)
