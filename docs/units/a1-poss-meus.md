## Outcomes

- **Complete the "my" possessive system:** meu/minha/meus/minhas
- Talk about family members in plural (meus irmãos, minhas irmãs, meus pais)
- Express possession of multiple items (meus livros, minhas chaves)
- Understand full gender AND number agreement for possessives
- Apply all four forms correctly based on the noun

## Vocabulary

- **meus/minhas** (my - masculine/feminine PLURAL) - extending Unit 14's meu/minha
- Plural family/friends:
  - irmãos/irmãs (brothers/sisters)
  - filhos/filhas (sons/daughters)
  - amigos/amigas (friends)
  - pais (parents - always masculine plural)
- Personal items (plural):
  - livros (books) - masculine plural
  - chaves (keys) - feminine plural
  - documentos (documents) - masculine plural
  - sapatos (shoes) - masculine plural

## Grammar

- **Extending Unit 14:** Adding PLURAL forms meus/minhas to complete "my" system
- **Four forms total for "my":**
  - Masculine singular: **meu** pai (from Unit 14)
  - Feminine singular: **minha** mãe (from Unit 14)
  - Masculine plural: **meus** irmãos (NEW!)
  - Feminine plural: **minhas** irmãs (NEW!)
- **Agreement rule (review from Unit 14):** Possessive agrees with the THING POSSESSED
- Position: possessive + noun (no article needed)

### Exemplar sentences

- Review: Meu pai é brasileiro (My father is Brazilian - from Unit 14)
- Review: Minha mãe trabalha no hospital (My mother works at the hospital - from Unit 14)
- **NEW:** Meus irmãos moram em São Paulo (My brothers live in São Paulo)
- **NEW:** Minhas irmãs falam inglês (My sisters speak English)
- **NEW:** Meus pais moram no Rio (My parents live in Rio)
- **NEW:** Meus amigos são brasileiros (My friends are Brazilian)
- **NEW:** Minhas chaves estão aqui (My keys are here)
- **NEW:** Meus livros são novos (My books are new)
