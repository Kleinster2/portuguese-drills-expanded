## Outcomes

- Use all three irregular imperfect verbs (ser, ter, vir)
- Describe what people/things were like in the past (era)
- Talk about age in the past (tinha X anos)
- Talk about possession in the past (tinha um carro)
- Talk about habitual coming in the past (vinha)
- Combine with regular imperfect verbs for complete past descriptions
- Tell sophisticated stories with complete imperfect tense knowledge

## Vocabulary

- **Time/age expressions with imperfect:**
  - anos (years - for age)
  - idade (age)
  - jovem (young)
  - velho/a (old)
- **Additional descriptive vocabulary:**
  - pequeno/a (small/little)
  - grande (big/large)
  - rico/a (rich)
  - pobre (poor)
  - feliz (happy)
  - triste (sad)
  - forte (strong)
  - fraco/a (weak)

## Grammar

- **Only THREE irregular verbs in imperfect: ser, ter, vir**
- **ser (to be) - imperfect:**
  - eu **era**, você/ele/ela **era**, nós **éramos**, vocês/eles/elas **eram**
- **ter (to have) - imperfect:**
  - eu **tinha**, você/ele/ela **tinha**, nós **tínhamos**, vocês/eles/elas **tinham**
- **vir (to come) - imperfect:**
  - eu **vinha**, você/ele/ela **vinha**, nós **vínhamos**, vocês/eles/elas **vinham**
- **These are the ONLY irregular verbs in imperfect tense!**

### Exemplar sentences

- Era alto (I/he was tall)
- Eu era jovem (I was young)
- Quando era criança, era pequeno (When I was a child, I was small)
- A casa era grande (The house was big)
- Eles eram ricos (They were rich)
- Tinha 20 anos (I was 20 years old)
- Não tinha dinheiro (I didn't have money)
- Tínhamos uma casa (We had a house)
- Vinha aqui todos os dias (I used to come here every day)
- Eles vinham sempre (They always used to come)
- Vínhamos de ônibus (We used to come by bus)
