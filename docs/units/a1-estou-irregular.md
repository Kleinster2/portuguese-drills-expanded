## Outcomes

- Express how they feel right now (temporary)
- Say where they are currently (not where they live - that's moro)
- Understand the ser vs estar distinction
- Distinguish permanent (sou) from temporary (estou)

## Vocabulary

- estou (I am) - **IRREGULAR** verb, second "to be" verb
- Temporary states/feelings:
  - cansado/cansada (tired)
  - feliz (happy)
  - triste (sad)
  - doente (sick)
  - ocupado/ocupada (busy)
  - com fome (hungry)
  - com sede (thirsty)
- Current location:
  - em casa (at home)
  - no trabalho (at work)
  - na escola (at school)
- agora (now), hoje (today)

## Grammar

- Second irregular verb: estou (I am - temporary/location)
- **Critical distinction: ser vs estar**
  - **ser (sou)** = permanent/inherent qualities, identity
  - **estar (estou)** = temporary states, current location, conditions
- Temporary states require estar, not ser
- Current location uses estar

### Exemplar sentences

- Estou cansado (I'm tired right now)
- Estou feliz hoje (I'm happy today)
- Estou em casa (I'm at home now)
- Estou no trabalho (I'm at work)
- Estou com fome (I'm hungry)
- Estou doente (I'm sick)
