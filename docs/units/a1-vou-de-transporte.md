## Outcomes

- Express going by a method of transport
- Use the irregular verb vou
- Distinguish between "ando de" and "vou de"

## Vocabulary

- vou (I go) - **IRREGULAR** verb
- Same transports from Unit 7: ônibus, bicicleta, carro

## Grammar

- First irregular motion verb: vou (I go)
- "Vou de" + transport (I go by [transport])
- Parallel structure to Unit 7's "ando de"

### Exemplar sentences

- Eu vou de ônibus (I go by bus)
- Eu vou de bicicleta (I go by bicycle)
- Eu vou de carro (I go by car)
- Eu vou a pé (I go on foot)
