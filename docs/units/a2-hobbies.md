## Outcomes

- Name 19 common hobbies and leisure activities
- Use JOGAR correctly for sports/games
- Use TOCAR correctly for instruments
- Express hobby preferences: "Eu gosto de ler"
- Combine hobbies with frequency adverbs

## Vocabulary

- **Sports and activities:**
  - jogar futebol (to play soccer)
  - jogar vôlei (to play volleyball)
  - jogar basquete (to play basketball)
  - nadar (to swim)
  - correr (to run)
  - caminhar (to walk/hike)
  - dançar (to dance)
  - cantar (to sing)

- **Leisure activities:**
  - ler (to read)
  - escrever (to write)
  - desenhar (to draw)
  - pintar (to paint)
  - tocar (to play instrument)
  - ouvir música (to listen to music)
  - assistir TV (to watch TV)
  - assistir filmes (to watch movies)
  - jogar videogame (to play video games)
  - viajar (to travel)
  - cozinhar (to cook)

## Grammar

- **JOGAR vs TOCAR - Critical distinction:**
  - **JOGAR** = play SPORTS/GAMES
    - jogar futebol (play soccer)
    - jogar vôlei (play volleyball)
    - jogar videogame (play video games)
    - jogar cartas (play cards)
  - **TOCAR** = play MUSICAL INSTRUMENTS
    - tocar violão (play guitar)
    - tocar piano (play piano)
    - tocar bateria (play drums)
  - **Do NOT confuse these two!**

- **GOSTAR DE + infinitive (to like to...):**
  - Eu gosto de ler. (I like to read.)
  - Ela gosta de nadar. (She likes to swim.)
  - Structure: gostar de + infinitive verb

- **Frequency with hobbies:**
  - Combining frequency adverbs (Unit 63) with hobbies:
  - Eu **sempre** jogo futebol. (I always play soccer.)
  - Eu **às vezes** leio. (I sometimes read.)

- **No article with sports after JOGAR:**
  - ✅ Eu jogo futebol. (I play soccer.) - NO article
  - ❌ Eu jogo o futebol. (wrong - don't use article)
  - Exception: specific game/match uses article
  - "Eu vou jogar o jogo" (I'm going to play the game - specific)

### Exemplar sentences

- Eu jogo futebol todo sábado. (I play soccer every Saturday.)
- Eu gosto de ler livros. (I like to read books.)
- Ela toca piano. (She plays piano.)
- Eu gosto de assistir filmes. (I like to watch movies.)
- Nós jogamos vôlei na praia. (We play volleyball on the beach.)
- Eu corro todos os dias. (I run every day.)
- Eu ouço música quando estudo. (I listen to music when I study.)
