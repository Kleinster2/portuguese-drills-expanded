## Outcomes

- Talk about past identity/professions (fui)
- Talk about where they went (fui)
- Distinguish ser/ir by context
- Talk about past possession (tive)
- Talk about past location/state (estive)
- Tell complete stories combining regular and irregular verbs

## Vocabulary

- Time expressions (review): ontem, na semana passada, no ano passado
- Additional time expressions:
  - no passado (in the past)
  - quando (when)
  - antes (before)

## Grammar

- **Preterite tense for the most essential irregular verbs**
- **AMAZING FACT: ser (to be) and ir (to go) share identical past tense forms!**
- Four essential irregulars:
  - **ser/ir**: fui, foi, fomos, foram
  - **ter**: tive, teve, tivemos, tiveram
  - **estar**: estive, esteve, estivemos, estiveram
- Context determines meaning for ser vs ir (both use fui, foi, etc.)
- These verbs don't follow regular patterns - must be memorized

### Exemplar sentences

- Fui professor no Brasil (I was a teacher in Brazil) - ser
- Fui ao cinema ontem (I went to the movies yesterday) - ir
- Ela foi brasileira (She was Brazilian) - doesn't work! Identity doesn't change
- Ele foi ao banco (He went to the bank) - ir
- Fomos ao restaurante (We went to the restaurant) - ir
- Tive um cachorro (I had a dog)
- Não tive tempo (I didn't have time)
- Estive em São Paulo (I was in São Paulo)
- Estivemos cansados (We were tired)
