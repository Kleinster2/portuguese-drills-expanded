## Outcomes

- Ask questions about any topic covered so far
- Use interrogatives with present, future, and past tenses
- Form information questions (not just yes/no questions)
- Understand that many question words they've seen informally are now systematic
- Conduct two-way conversations (not just answer, but also ask)

## Vocabulary

- **Question words (interrogatives):**
  - **o que / que** (what)
  - **quem** (who)
  - **onde** (where)
  - **quando** (when)
  - **como** (how)
  - **por que** (why)
  - **qual / quais** (which / which ones)
  - **quanto/a/os/as** (how much / how many)

## Grammar

- **Systematic introduction to interrogatives**
- Question formation with all tenses (present, future, past)
- Word order: interrogative + verb + subject (or just interrogative + verb with pro-drop)
- Using question words students have encountered informally since early units
- Questions can end with question mark but intonation is key

### Exemplar sentences

- O que você faz? (What do you do?)
- Quem é ela? (Who is she?)
- Onde você mora? (Where do you live?)
- Quando você vai viajar? (When are you going to travel?)
- Como você está? (How are you?)
- Por que você gosta de português? (Why do you like Portuguese?)
- Qual é seu nome? (What is your name? / literally: Which is your name?)
- Quanto custa? (How much does it cost?)
- Quantos irmãos você tem? (How many siblings do you have?)
