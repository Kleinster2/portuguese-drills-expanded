## Outcomes

- **Create negative sentences with ANY verb** - major milestone!
- Express what they DON'T do, DON'T have, AREN'T, etc.
- Understand that negation is universal across all verbs
- Use "não" as the PRIMARY negation word in Portuguese
- Apply this pattern to all future verbs automatically

## Vocabulary

- **não** (not/no) - **CRITICAL UNIVERSAL CONCEPT**
- Review all verbs learned so far to apply negation

## Grammar

- **NEGATION: "Não" + verb** - UNIVERSAL PATTERN for negative sentences!
  - Pattern: **não + verb = negative**
  - Works with ALL verbs learned so far
  - Simple placement: não comes directly before the verb
- This is a FOUNDATIONAL concept that applies to EVERY verb in Portuguese

### Exemplar sentences

- Não sou americano (I'm not American)
- Não moro no Rio (I don't live in Rio)
- Não trabalho no banco (I don't work at the bank)
- Não falo espanhol (I don't speak Spanish)
- Não ando de carro (I don't go by car)
- Não vou de ônibus (I don't go by bus)
- Não estou cansado (I'm not tired)
- Não estou em casa (I'm not at home)
