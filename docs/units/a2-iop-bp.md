## Outcomes

- Express to/for whom actions are directed
- Use verbs like dar, dizer, mostrar with indirect objects
- Distinguish between direct objects (Unit 36) and indirect objects (Unit 40)
- Understand that me/te/nos can be direct OR indirect (context determines)
- Form more sophisticated sentences with both direct and indirect objects

## Vocabulary

- **Indirect object pronouns:**
  - **lhe** (to/for him, to/for her, to/for you - formal)
  - **lhes** (to/for them, to/for you plural)
  - Review: **me** (to/for me), **te** (to/for you - informal), **nos** (to/for us)
- **Verbs commonly used with indirect objects:**
  - dar (to give - Unit 33)
  - dizer (to say/tell - Unit 39)
  - falar (to speak - Unit 1)
  - perguntar (to ask)
  - mostrar (to show)
  - contar (to tell/count)
  - explicar (to explain)
  - enviar (to send)
  - emprestar (to lend)
  - pedir (to ask for/request)
- **Additional vocabulary:**
  - ajuda (help)
  - favor (favor)
  - dinheiro (money)
  - livro (book)
  - telefone (phone/phone number)
  - endereço (address)
  - resposta (answer)

## Grammar

- **Indirect object pronouns: replace "to/for someone" (recipient of action)**
- **Key distinction from direct objects (Unit 36):**
  - **Direct object:** WHO/WHAT receives the action directly (no preposition)
  - **Indirect object:** TO/FOR WHOM the action is done (with preposition a/para)
- **In Brazilian Portuguese, pronouns go BEFORE the verb**
- Pattern: subject + indirect pronoun + verb + (direct object)
  - Eu **lhe** dei o livro (I gave the book to him/her)
  - Ele **me** disse a verdade (He told the truth to me = He told me the truth)
  - Ela **nos** mostrou as fotos (She showed the photos to us)
- Indirect object answers "to whom?" or "for whom?"
  - Dei o livro a Maria → Dei a quem? A Maria → **Lhe** dei o livro (I gave the book to her)
  - Falei com João → Falei com quem? Com João → **Lhe** falei (I spoke to him)

### Exemplar sentences

- Lhe dei o livro (I gave the book to him/her)
- Ele me disse a verdade (He told me the truth)
- Ela nos mostrou o caminho (She showed us the way)
- Vou lhe contar uma história (I'm going to tell a story to you/him/her)
- Eles me perguntaram tudo (They asked me everything)
- Lhe pedi ajuda (I asked him/her for help)
- Me empresta dinheiro? (Can you lend me money? / Will you lend money to me?)
