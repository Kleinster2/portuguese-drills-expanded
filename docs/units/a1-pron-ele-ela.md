## Outcomes

- Talk about people not in the conversation (third person)
- Distinguish between ele (he) and ela (she) based on gender
- Recognize that você/ele/ela all share the same verb forms
- Ask and answer questions about other people

## Vocabulary

- ele (he)
- ela (she)
- o nome (the name)
- Common names and relationships (for practice examples)

## Grammar

- **ele** = he / **ela** = she (third person singular)
- **KEY INSIGHT: ele/ela use the SAME verb forms as você!**
- Gender distinction: ele (masculine) vs ela (feminine)
- Verb forms (identical to você):
  - -AR verbs: ele/ela mora, trabalha, anda, fala, gosta (ending in -a)
  - -ER verbs: ele/ela come, bebe, corre (ending in -e)
  - Irregular: ele/ela **é**, **está**, tem
- Talking about a third person (not present in the conversation)

### Exemplar sentences

- Ele é brasileiro. (He is Brazilian.)
- Ela mora em São Paulo. (She lives in São Paulo.)
- Ele trabalha na universidade. (He works at the university.)
- Ela fala português e inglês. (She speaks Portuguese and English.)
- Ele está cansado. (He is tired.)
- Ela tem um cachorro. (She has a dog.)
- Ele gosta de café. (He likes coffee.)
