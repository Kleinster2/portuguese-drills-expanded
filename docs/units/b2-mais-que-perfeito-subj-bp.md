## Outcomes

After this unit, the student can recognize and form the **mais-que-perfeito do subjuntivo** (compound past subjunctive) and use it as the subjuntivo clause in counterfactual past conditionals.

## Vocabulary

- **tivesse / houvesse + particípio** — pluperfect subjunctive auxiliary + participle
- *houvesse* is the more formal/written alternative; *tivesse* dominates in spoken BP

## Grammar

Formed with imperfeito subjuntivo of *ter* (or *haver*) + past participle:

| Pessoa | Auxiliar | + particípio |
|---|---|---|
| eu | tivesse | falado |
| você / ele / ela | tivesse | falado |
| nós | tivéssemos | falado |
| vocês / eles / elas | tivessem | falado |

### Minimal example

- *Se eu **tivesse estudado** mais, teria passado.* — If I had studied more, I would have passed.

The form's primary use is in Type 3 (counterfactual past) conditionals — see `b2-cond-contra-bp`.
