## Outcomes

- Use ordinal numbers for floors (1st-10th)
- Use "primeiro" for 1st of any month (from Unit 52)
- Express positions and rankings
- Describe sequences (first time, second attempt, etc.)
- Understand ordinal abbreviations (1º, 2ª, 3º)
- Apply correct gender agreement

## Vocabulary

- **Ordinal numbers 1st-10th:**
  - primeiro/primeira (first - 1st)
  - segundo/segunda (second - 2nd)
  - terceiro/terceira (third - 3rd)
  - quarto/quarta (fourth - 4th)
  - quinto/quinta (fifth - 5th)
  - sexto/sexta (sixth - 6th)
  - sétimo/sétima (seventh - 7th)
  - oitavo/oitava (eighth - 8th)
  - nono/nona (ninth - 9th)
  - décimo/décima (tenth - 10th)
- **andar** (floor - in building)
- **lugar** (place, position)
- **posição** (position)
- **classificação** (ranking, classification)

## Grammar

- **Ordinal numbers have gender agreement:** primeiro/primeira, segundo/segunda
- **Used for:**
  - Floors: "primeiro andar" (first floor), "terceiro andar" (third floor)
  - Dates: "primeiro de maio" (May 1st) - ONLY for 1st of month!
  - Positions: "primeiro lugar" (first place)
  - Sequences: "a primeira vez" (the first time)
  - Royalty: "Dom Pedro Segundo" (Dom Pedro the Second)
- **Position:** ordinal + noun (like other adjectives)
- **Abbreviations:** 1º (masc), 1ª (fem), 2º, 3º, etc.
- **After 10th:** Usually use cardinal numbers instead
  - o décimo primeiro andar (11th floor - formal)
  - o andar onze (floor 11 - more common)

### Exemplar sentences

- Moro no segundo andar (I live on the second floor)
- O escritório fica no quinto andar (The office is on the fifth floor)
- Esta é minha primeira vez aqui (This is my first time here)
- Ele é o terceiro da fila (He's third in line)
- A segunda porta à esquerda (The second door on the left)
- O primeiro dia de aula (The first day of class)
- Fiquei em quarto lugar (I came in fourth place)
- Vou pela terceira vez (I'm going for the third time)
