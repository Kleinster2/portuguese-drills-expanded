## Summary Statistics

### Question Types
- **Comprehension:** 7 questions (21.9%)
- **Production:** 25 questions (78.1%)

### Chip Analysis
- **Average chips per question:** 10 options
- **Most common incorrect chips:** Different verb tenses, similar conjugations
- **Pedagogical design:** Chips include plausible distractors from different:
  - Verb persons (sou/és/é/somos/são)
  - Verb tenses (present/past/future)
  - Similar verbs (ser/estar, morar/ficar)
  - Related prepositions (de/do/da/em/no/na)

### Chip Design Principles Observed
1. **First chip = correct answer** (in most production questions)
2. **Similar forms included** (to test understanding, not just recognition)
3. **Tense variations** (present + past + future forms)
4. **Person variations** (eu/você/ele forms for same verb)
5. **Minimal pairs** (do/da/dos/das, no/na/nos)
