# B2 Portuguese Curriculum - Upper Intermediate

## Overview

B2 represents independent user level where learners can understand the main ideas of complex text, interact with a degree of fluency and spontaneity, and produce clear, detailed text on a wide range of subjects. Learners can explain viewpoints on topical issues giving advantages and disadvantages.

All drills support both Brazilian Portuguese (BP) and European Portuguese (EP).

---

## Advanced Subjunctive

### 1. Future Subjunctive
- Formation: preterite stem + -r (falar, comer, partir)
- Irregular: for, tiver, fizer, puder, vier, souber
- Temporal clauses: quando eu falar, assim que ele chegar
- Conditional clauses: se você quiser, se ela puder
- Relative clauses: quem quiser, o que você preferir

### 2. Subjunctive in All Contexts
- Review of all three subjunctives
- Choosing correct subjunctive tense
- Subjunctive vs indicative in subtle contexts
- Literary uses

### 3. Conditional Sentences (Full System)
- Real conditions: Se chover, fico em casa (future subj)
- Hypothetical present: Se eu fosse rico, viajaria (imperfect subj)
- Hypothetical past: Se eu tivesse estudado, teria passado
- Mixed conditionals

---

## Advanced Verb Forms

### 4. Personal Infinitive
- Formation: infinitive + personal endings (-es, -mos, -em)
- When to use: different subjects, after prepositions
- "Para eu fazer" vs "Para fazer"
- Unique to Portuguese among Romance languages

### 5. All Compound Tenses
- Future perfect: terei falado
- Conditional perfect: teria falado
- Past perfect subjunctive: tivesse falado
- Future perfect subjunctive: tiver falado
- When to use each

### 6. Progressive Forms in All Tenses
- Estava falando, estive falando
- Estarei falando, estaria falando
- BP vs EP: estar + gerund vs estar a + infinitive

### 7. Double Participles
- Regular vs irregular participles
- Ter/haver + regular (tenho aceitado)
- Ser/estar + irregular (foi aceito)
- Common verbs: aceitar/aceito, eleger/eleito, pagar/pago

## Complex Clause Structures

### 8. Advanced Relative Clauses
- Cujo/cuja/cujos/cujas (whose)
- O qual, a qual, os quais, as quais
- Preposition + relative pronoun combinations
- Defining vs non-defining clauses

### 9. Concessive Clauses
- Embora, ainda que, mesmo que + subjunctive
- Apesar de + infinitive
- Por mais que, por muito que

### 10. Purpose & Result Clauses
- Para que + subjunctive
- A fim de que + subjunctive
- De modo que, de forma que (+ indicative/subjunctive)
- Tão...que, tanto...que (result)

### 11. Conditional Conjunctions
- Caso + subjunctive (in case)
- Desde que + subjunctive (provided that)
- A não ser que (unless)
- Contanto que (as long as)

## Summary

B2 covers approximately 23 topic areas focusing on:
- Future subjunctive and complete conditional system
- Personal infinitive (unique to Portuguese)
- All compound tenses and their uses
- Complex clause structures
- Deep BP vs EP comparison
- Advanced communication and argumentation
- Specialized registers (academic, business, media)

At B2 completion, learners have the foundation for C1 (advanced) study and can function effectively in Portuguese-speaking professional and academic environments.
