## Verb System

### Saber: Aspectual Shift — soube vs sabia
- **Outcome:** Distinguish **soube** (found out, learned at a specific moment) from **sabia** (knew, was already aware)
- **Grammar:** soube: = found out, learned, discovered → marks the moment knowledge was acquired
- **Unit:** [[b1-saber-aspect-bp]]

### Conhecer: Aspectual Shift — conheci vs conhecia
- **Outcome:** Distinguish **conheci** (met for the first time / went to a place for the first time) from **conhecia** (was acquainted with, was familiar with)
- **Grammar:** conheci: = met (a person for the first time) / visited (a place for the first time) → punctual event of acquaintance
- **Unit:** [[b1-conhecer-aspect-bp]]

### Poder: Aspectual Shift — pude vs podia
- **Outcome:** Distinguish **pude** (managed to, succeeded in doing on a specific occasion) from **podia** (was able to, had the general ability or context)
- **Grammar:** pude: = managed to / succeeded in → completed action, specific moment, often after effort
- **Unit:** [[b1-poder-aspect-bp]]

### Querer: Aspectual Shift — quis vs queria
- **Outcome:** Distinguish **quis** (acted on wanting / attempted / in negation, refused) from **queria** (wanted as an ongoing state, often unrealized)
- **Grammar:** quis: = tried to / acted on wanting → punctual action driven by desire; in the negative, "refused"
- **Unit:** [[b1-querer-aspect-bp]]

### Pôr e seus derivados (compor, propor, expor, dispor...)
- **Outcome:** Conjugate any verb derived from **pôr** by inheriting the parent's irregular paradigm — present, preterite, imperfeito, futuro, condicional, subjuntivo
- **Grammar:** *O comitê **propôs** uma nova solução.* — The committee proposed a new solution.
- **Unit:** [[b1-por-derivados]]

### Ter e seus derivados (manter, conter, obter, deter, reter, abster-se)
- **Outcome:** Conjugate any verb derived from **ter** by inheriting the parent's irregular paradigm
- **Grammar:** *A caixa **contém** vinte livros.* — The box contains twenty books.
- **Unit:** [[b1-ter-derivados]]

### Vir e seus derivados (intervir, convir, provir, advir, sobrevir)
- **Outcome:** Conjugate any verb derived from **vir** by inheriting the parent's irregular paradigm
- **Grammar:** *Convém que tu **venhas** cedo.* — It's advisable for you to come early.
- **Unit:** [[b1-vir-derivados]]

- **Outcome:** Use verbs of doubt and uncertainty (*duvidar, não acreditar, não pensar*) with subjuntivo presente

### Subjuntivo após conjunções subordinativas

- **Unit:** [[b1-subj-trigger-conjuncoes]]

### Subjuntivo em construções hipotéticas (se + imperfeito subjuntivo)
- **Outcome:** Form hypothetical conditions of the *Type 2* shape: **se + imperfeito subjuntivo + condicional**
- **Grammar:** *Se eu **fosse** rico, **viajaria** pelo mundo.* — If I were rich, I'd travel the world.
- **Unit:** [[b1-subj-trigger-hipotetico]]

### Subjuntivo em orações relativas com antecedente indefinido ou inexistente
- **Outcome:** Distinguish definite-antecedent relative clauses (indicative) from indefinite or non-existent antecedent clauses (subjuntivo)
- **Grammar:** *Procuro alguém que **fale** francês.* — I'm looking for someone who speaks French. (indefinite — anyone qualifying)
- **Unit:** [[b1-subj-trigger-relativo]]

### Condicional Tipo 1 — Real / provável (se + indicativo)
- **Outcome:** Construct **Type 1 (real / likely)** conditional sentences in BP using the indicative pair: `se + presente indicativo + presente / futuro indicativo`
- **Grammar:** *Se chove, eu fico em casa.* — If it rains, I stay home. (general / habitual)
- **Unit:** [[b1-cond-real-bp]]

## Grammar

### Crase (à, às)
- **Outcome:** Use crase correctly with feminine nouns
- **Grammar:** Crase:: contraction of a (preposition) + a (article) = à
- **Unit:** [[b1-crase]]

### Pronome relativo *que* — pronome relativo padrão
- **Outcome:** Use **que** as the default relative pronoun for both subject and object positions, with both human and non-human antecedents
- **Grammar:** *O homem **que mora ao lado** é simpático.* — The man who lives next door is nice. (restrictive — *which* man)
- **Unit:** [[b1-relativo-que]]

### Pronome relativo *quem* — antecedente humano após preposição
- **Outcome:** Use **quem** as the [+human] relative pronoun after a preposition (*de quem, com quem, para quem, sobre quem, a quem, em quem*)
- **Grammar:** *A pessoa **de quem** te falei mora em Coimbra.* — The person I told you about lives in Coimbra.
- **Unit:** [[b1-relativo-quem]]

### Pronome relativo *cujo* — possessivo (concorda com o possuído)
- **Outcome:** Use **cujo / cuja / cujos / cujas** as the possessive relative pronoun ("whose")
- **Grammar:** *A mulher **cujo nome** esqueci.* — The woman whose name I forgot.
- **Unit:** [[b1-relativo-cujo]]

### Pronomes relativos *o qual* (formal) e *onde* (locativo)
- **Outcome:** Use **o qual / a qual / os quais / as quais** as a formal alternative to *que*, especially after prepositions and in disambiguation contexts
- **Grammar:** *Trabalho num projeto **sobre o qual** escrevi um artigo.* (formal) — I work on a project I wrote an article about.
- **Unit:** [[b1-relativo-formal-locativo]]

### Voz passiva analítica (ser + particípio)
- **Outcome:** Transform active sentences into the analytic passive (**ser + particípio**) preserving tense, person, and agreement
- **Grammar:** *O livro foi **escrito** pela Ana.* — masc sg
- **Unit:** [[b1-passiva-ser]]

### Passiva sintética / impessoal (verbo + se)
- **Outcome:** Use the **passive-se** construction for generic statements, instructions, commercial signs, and impersonal contexts where no agent is mentioned
- **Grammar:** *Vendem-se livros.* — Books are sold (here / by us, no agent mentionable).
- **Unit:** [[b1-passiva-se]]

### Discurso direto vs discurso indireto (declarativo)
- **Outcome:** Transform direct quotations into reported (indirect) speech using verbs of saying (*dizer, comentar, afirmar, contar, responder*)
- **Grammar:** Direct: *"Vou ao cinema."* → Reported: *Ele diz que vai ao cinema.* (no shift)
- **Unit:** [[b1-discurso-direto-indireto]]

### Perguntas indiretas (yes/no com *se*, conteúdo com wh-words)
- **Outcome:** Convert direct yes/no questions into indirect questions using **se** (whether)
- **Grammar:** *"Onde mora ele?"* (inverted, somewhat formal)
- **Unit:** [[b1-perguntas-indiretas]]
