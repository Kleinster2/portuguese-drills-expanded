A. Vocabulário — Estrutura da ementa

a ementa — menu
a ementa do dia — daily set menu (fixed price, often includes drink and dessert)
o prato do dia — dish of the day
a especialidade da casa — house special
a secção — menu section
as entradas — starters • as sopas — soups
os peixes — fish (section) • as carnes — meat (section)
as sobremesas — desserts • as bebidas — drinks • os vinhos — wines
uma dose — full portion (often serves 1–2)
uma meia dose — half portion (one person; ~60–70% of full price)
o couvert — bread, olives, butter, sometimes cheese, brought to the table (usually charged)
B. Vocabulário — Modos de preparação

grelhado/a — grilled
assado/a — roasted (oven or open fire)
frito/a — fried
cozido/a — boiled
no forno — baked (in the oven)
na brasa — over coals
à brás — "Brás-style" (with shredded fish, eggs, shoestring fries)
à lagareiro — "Lagareiro-style" (roasted with olive oil and crushed potatoes)
à portuguesa — "Portuguese-style" (traditional preparation; varies by dish)
C. Vocabulário — Pratos típicos a conhecer

bacalhau com natas — salt cod baked with cream and potatoes (natas = cream)

sardinhas assadas — grilled sardines (summer dish)
polvo à lagareiro — octopus roasted with olive oil and crushed potatoes
cozido à portuguesa — boiled meats, sausages, cabbage and vegetables
pastel de bacalhau — fried salt-cod fritter (entrada or snack)
francesinha — layered sandwich with melted cheese and beer-tomato sauce (Porto)

Dose, meia dose e couvert
Dose: a full portion. Often generous — pode dar para duas pessoas (can serve two).
Meia dose: a half portion. Costs about 60–70% of a full dose. Ideal for one person eating moderately.
Couvert: bread, olives, butter, sometimes cheese, brought to the table before the meal. It is not free by default. If you don't want it, say "Não, obrigado/a" (No, thank you) when it arrives.
Pedir uma cerveja
Em Lisboa: uma imperial (a small draft beer, ~20cl)
No norte / Porto: um fino (the same small draft, called fino north of Coimbra)
Em garrafa: uma cerveja (a bottled beer; mini = 20cl, normal = 33cl)
O que provar — Pratos por região

Açores: lapas grelhadas (small rock-clinging shellfish, grilled with garlic butter), cozido das Furnas (meats and vegetables slow-cooked underground using volcanic steam, São Miguel), queijo de São Jorge (aged cow's-milk cheese), bifes de atum (steak-cut tuna), ananás dos Açores (small, intensely sweet greenhouse-grown pineapple)
Algarve: cataplana de marisco (seafood stew cooked in a hinged copper clamshell pan), amêijoas à Bulhão Pato (clams steamed with garlic, coriander, olive oil and white wine), peixe na brasa (grilled fish at beach restaurants)
Lisboa: pastéis de nata (small flaky-pastry custard tarts, lightly scorched on top), bifana (thinly sliced pork marinated in garlic and white wine, in a crusty roll), bifes à café (steak with mustard-butter sauce, café-style), ginjinha (sour-cherry liqueur, often served in a small chocolate cup)
Norte (Porto e Minho): francesinha (layered sandwich with cheese and beer-tomato sauce), tripas à moda do Porto (tripe stew with white beans and chouriço), vinho verde (young, lightly sparkling white wine from Minho)

C. Completa com o vocabulário das caixas

Preenche cada espaço com a palavra correta.
Exemplo: As sardinhas estão assadas.
1. I want to eat lightly. Can I order a smaller portion? → Quero comer pouco. Posso pedir uma ______ dose? (meia / dose / inteira)
2. Cod with egg, onion, and shoestring fries. → O bacalhau à ______ leva ovo, cebola e batata palha. (brás / lagareiro / portuguesa)
3. The potatoes are cooked in the oven with olive oil. → As batatas estão ______ no forno com azeite. (assadas / cozidas / fritas)
4. The bread and olives brought to the table are the ______. → O pão e as azeitonas trazidos para a mesa são o ______. (couvert / prato / secção)
5. The bill, please. → A ______, faz favor. (conta / ementa / gorjeta)
6. Octopus with smashed potatoes. → O polvo à ______ vem com batatas a murro. (lagareiro / brás / portuguesa)
7. Today's house dish is cozido à portuguesa. → Hoje a ______ da casa é o cozido à portuguesa. (especialidade / secção / dose)
8. Can I pay by card? → Posso pagar com ______? (cartão / troco / conta)
9. Sardines are cooked over the coals. → As sardinhas são feitas ______ brasa. (na / no / em)
10. This portion is enough for two people. → Esta dose ______ para duas pessoas. (dá / vai / leva) — "dar para" = to be enough for
