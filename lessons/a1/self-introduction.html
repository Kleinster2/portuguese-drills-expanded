STEP 1

Learn "Eu sou" (I am)

Eu sou

= I am

This is the most important phrase for introducing yourself. You'll use it to say your name, where you're from, what you do, and your nationality.

ðŸ‘¤
Eu = I (the person speaking)
ðŸ”—
sou = am (from the verb "ser" - to be)
Good news: You don't need to understand grammar yet! Just memorize "Eu sou" as a chunk and use it.
STEP 2

Say Your Name

Eu sou o/a + [your name]

o = for male names • a = for female names

Examples:

Eu sou o Daniel.

I am Daniel. (male name = o)

Eu sou a Sofia.

I am Sofia. (female name = a)

Tip: The "o/a" before names is optional in casual speech. You can also say "Eu sou Daniel" or "Eu sou Sofia".
STEP 3

Say Where You're From

Eu sou de + [city/country]

de = from

Examples:

Eu sou de Nova York.

I am from New York.

Eu sou do Brasil.

I am from Brazil. (do = de + o)

Eu sou da Argentina.

I am from Argentina. (da = de + a)

Note: Some countries use "do/da" (de + the): do Brasil, da Argentina, dos Estados Unidos. Cities usually don't: de Paris, de Tokyo.
STEP 4

Say Your Profession

Eu sou + [profession]

No article needed before professions!

Common Professions:

professor / professora
teacher
engenheiro / engenheira
engineer
estudante
student
advogado / advogada
lawyer

Examples:

Eu sou professor.

I am a teacher. (male)

Eu sou engenheira.

I am an engineer. (female)

Pattern: Many professions have male (-o) and female (-a) forms. Some like "estudante" work for both genders.
STEP 5

Say Your Nationality

Eu sou + [nationality]

Nationalities also have male/female forms!

Common Nationalities:

americano / americana
American
brasileiro / brasileira
Brazilian
espanhol / espanhola
Spanish
canadense
Canadian (same for m/f)

Examples:

Eu sou americano.

I am American. (male)

Eu sou brasileira.

I am Brazilian. (female)

PRACTICE

Put It All Together

Now combine everything into a complete self-introduction:

Eu sou a Maria.

I am Maria.

Eu sou de Los Angeles.

I am from Los Angeles.

Eu sou americana.

I am American.

Eu sou professora.

I am a teacher.
