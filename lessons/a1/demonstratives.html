Demonstrative Adjectives & Pronouns

STEP 1

Three Degrees of Distance

Portuguese has three demonstratives based on distance from speaker and listener (English only has two: this/that).

  • Este/Esta - near the speaker (this)
  • Esse/Essa - near the listener (that, nearby)
  • Aquele/Aquela - far from both (that, over there)
