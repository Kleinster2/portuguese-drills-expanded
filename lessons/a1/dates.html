STEP 1

Days of the Week

Portuguese days are unique! Most come from "feira" (fair/market day) instead of gods like in English.

STEP 3

Expressing Dates

Portuguese dates use cardinal numbers (not ordinals like English "the 5th").

[day] de [month] de [year]

day + "of" + month + "of" + year

Examples:

15 de marco de 2024

March 15, 2024

25 de dezembro

December 25 (Christmas)

1 de janeiro

January 1 (New Year's Day)

Exception: The 1st of the month uses the ordinal: primeiro de janeiro (not "um de janeiro").
