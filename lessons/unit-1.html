Grammar Patterns

Pattern 1.1: Eu sou [o/a] [name]

Introducing yourself by name
Eu sou + [o/a] + name
All steps sound the same

I am Daniel. (masculine)
Eu sou o/u/ Daniel/u/.
Eu sou u Danieu.
Feminine example (no changes):
Eu sou a Sofia. - I am Sofia.

Pattern 1.2: Eu sou [profession]
