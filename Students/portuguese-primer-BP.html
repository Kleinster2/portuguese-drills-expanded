seu = your (SÊH-oo) — used with você.
faz = do/make (3rd person sing., FAH-ys).
trabalhar = to work (trah-bah-ly-AHR); você trabalha (voh-SÊH trah-BAH-ly-ah).
morar = to live; você mora (voh-SÊH MÓH-rah).

5. Transportes — Transportation

ir de = go by • andar de = ride (by) • passear de = ride around (in/on)
sempre = always • nunca = never
ontem = yesterday • na semana passada = last week • no mês passado = last month • no ano passado = last year

7. Possessivos — Possessive Adjectives

Possessives agree with the thing possessed, not the possessor. In BP, dele/dela is commonly used instead of seu/sua to avoid ambiguity.
EnglishMasc. Sing.Fem. Sing.Masc. Pl.Fem. Pl.
mymeuminhameusminhas
your (você)seusuaseussuas
hisdeledeledeledele
herdeladeladeladela
ournossonossanossosnossas
their (masc.)delesdelesdelesdeles
their (fem.)delasdelasdelasdelas
Usage note: dele/dela/deles/delas come after the noun: “o carro dele” (his car), “a casa dela” (her house). These are invariable — they don’t change for the gender/number of the thing possessed, only for the possessor.
