# Phase 1 Syllabus - Self-Introduction (Eu)

**Goal:** Students master complete self-introduction using first person (eu) only.

**Method:** Verb-by-verb exhaustion. Master all statements with one verb before moving to the next.

**Verb Sequence:**
1. Eu sou
2. Eu moro
3. Eu trabalho
4. Eu falo
5. Eu gosto

**After Phase 1:** Introduce ele/ela (describing others)

---

## Verb 1: Eu sou (to be - permanent/identity)

Students should be able to produce these statement patterns:

### Pattern 1.1: Eu sou o/a [name]
- **Structure:** Eu sou + definite article + name
- **Gender:** o (masculine) / a (feminine)
- **Usage:** Introducing yourself by name

### Pattern 1.2: Eu sou [profession]
- **Structure:** Eu sou + profession noun
- **Gender agreement:** Profession changes for masculine/feminine
- **Usage:** Stating your profession

### Pattern 1.3: Eu sou [nationality]
- **Structure:** Eu sou + nationality adjective
- **Gender agreement:** Nationality changes for masculine/feminine
- **Usage:** Stating your nationality

### Pattern 1.4: Eu sou de [city]
- **Structure:** Eu sou de + city name
- **Preposition:** de (from)
- **Usage:** Stating city of origin

### Pattern 1.5: Eu sou do/da/dos/das [place]
- **Structure:** Eu sou + de+article + place name
- **Contractions:**
  - de + o = do (masculine singular)
  - de + a = da (feminine singular)
  - de + os = dos (masculine plural)
  - de + as = das (feminine plural)
- **Usage:** Stating country/region of origin (when place has article)

### Pattern 1.6: Eu sou [characteristic]
- **Structure:** Eu sou + adjective
- **Gender agreement:** Adjective changes for masculine/feminine
- **Usage:** Stating permanent characteristics (tall, short, etc.)

## Verb 2: Eu moro (to live/reside)

Students should be able to produce these statement patterns:

### Pattern 2.1: Eu moro em [city]
- **Structure:** Eu moro em + city name
- **Preposition:** em (in)
- **Usage:** Stating where you live (city without article)

### Pattern 2.2: Eu moro no/na/nos/nas [place]
- **Structure:** Eu moro + em+article + place name
- **Contractions:**
  - em + o = no (masculine singular)
  - em + a = na (feminine singular)
  - em + os = nos (masculine plural)
  - em + as = nas (feminine plural)
- **Usage:** Stating where you live (country/place with article)

### Pattern 2.3: Eu moro na rua [street name]
- **Structure:** Eu moro na rua + street name
- **Fixed:** "rua" is feminine, always "na rua"
- **Usage:** Stating your street

### Pattern 2.4: Eu moro na avenida [avenue name]
- **Structure:** Eu moro na avenida + avenue name
- **Fixed:** "avenida" is feminine, always "na avenida"
- **Usage:** Stating your avenue

### Pattern 2.5: Eu moro [location adverb]
- **Structure:** Eu moro + aqui/lá/longe/perto
- **Usage:** Stating relative location (here/there/far/near)

---

## Verb 3: Eu trabalho (to work)

Students should be able to produce these statement patterns:

### Pattern 3.1: Eu trabalho no/na [workplace]
- **Structure:** Eu trabalho + em+article + workplace noun
- **Contractions:** no (masculine) / na (feminine)
- **Gender:** Workplace noun determines contraction
- **Usage:** Stating where you work

### Pattern 3.2: Eu trabalho em [workplace without article]
- **Structure:** Eu trabalho em + workplace
- **Preposition:** em (at/in)
- **Usage:** When workplace doesn't take article

### Pattern 3.3: Eu trabalho [location adverb]
- **Structure:** Eu trabalho + aqui/lá
- **Usage:** Stating relative work location (here/there)

### Pattern 3.4: Eu trabalho como [profession]
- **Structure:** Eu trabalho como + profession
- **Preposition:** como (as)
- **Usage:** Stating what you work as (alternative to "Eu sou [profession]")

---

## Verb 4: Eu falo (to speak)

Students should be able to produce these statement patterns:

### Pattern 4.1: Eu falo [language]
- **Structure:** Eu falo + language name
- **No article:** Languages don't take articles in this construction
- **Usage:** Stating languages you speak

### Pattern 4.2: Eu falo [language] e [language]
- **Structure:** Eu falo + language + e + language
- **Conjunction:** e (and)
- **Usage:** Stating multiple languages

### Pattern 4.3: Eu sempre falo [language]
- **Structure:** Eu sempre falo + language
- **Adverb:** sempre (always)
- **Usage:** Emphasizing regular language use

### Pattern 4.4: Eu falo [language] com [person]
- **Structure:** Eu falo + language + com + person
- **Preposition:** com (with)
- **Usage:** Stating who you speak a language with

---

## Verb 5: Eu gosto (to like)

Students should be able to produce these statement patterns:

### Pattern 5.1: Eu gosto de [thing]
- **Structure:** Eu gosto de + noun (no article)
- **Fixed preposition:** de (of)
- **Usage:** Stating things you like (generic/uncountable)

### Pattern 5.2: Eu gosto do/da/dos/das [thing]
- **Structure:** Eu gosto + de+article + specific noun
- **Contractions:**
  - de + o = do (masculine singular)
  - de + a = da (feminine singular)
  - de + os = dos (masculine plural)
  - de + as = das (feminine plural)
- **Usage:** Stating specific things you like (with article)

### Pattern 5.3: Eu gosto de [infinitive verb]
- **Structure:** Eu gosto de + verb infinitive
- **Fixed preposition:** de
- **Usage:** Stating activities you like to do

---

## Summary

### Verbs Covered:
1. ✅ **Eu sou** - Identity, profession, origin, characteristics (6 patterns)
2. ✅ **Eu moro** - Residence location (5 patterns)
3. ✅ **Eu trabalho** - Work location and occupation (4 patterns)
4. ✅ **Eu falo** - Languages spoken (4 patterns)
5. ✅ **Eu gosto** - Preferences and likes (3 patterns)

### Total Patterns: 22

### Complete Self-Introduction After Phase 1:

Students can confidently introduce themselves:
```
Eu sou a Maria.                    (name)
Eu sou brasileira.                 (nationality)
Eu sou do Brasil.                  (origin)
Eu sou professora.                 (profession)
Eu sou alta.                       (characteristic)

Eu moro em São Paulo.              (city)
Eu moro na rua Paulista.           (street)

Eu trabalho no hospital.           (workplace)
Eu trabalho como médica.           (occupation)

Eu falo português e inglês.        (languages)

Eu gosto de música.                (preferences)
Eu gosto de estudar.               (activities)
```
