CATEGORY GUIDE
===============

POR Categories:
• reason/cause - because of, due to
• price/exchange - how much paid
• through space - movement through
• means/method - by email, by phone
• duration - for X hours/days
• on behalf of - in favor of someone
• passive agent - by whom (written BY)

PARA Categories:
• purpose/goal - what something is for
• destination - where going
• recipient - who receives
• deadline - by when, due date
• opinion - in my view
• direction - looking toward
• in order to - to do something
