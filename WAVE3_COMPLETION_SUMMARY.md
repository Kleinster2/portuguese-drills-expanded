#### **Unit 82: Present Subjunctive - Regular Verbs**
- **Drill:** present-subjunctive.json
- **Formation:** Opposite vowel rule
  - -AR verbs: -o ‚Üí -e endings (falo ‚Üí fale, falemos, falem)
  - -ER/-IR verbs: -o ‚Üí -a endings (como ‚Üí coma, comamos, comam)

#### **Unit 84: Imperfect Subjunctive**
- **Drill:** imperfect-subjunctive.json
- **Formation:** Preterite 3rd plural - ram + sse
